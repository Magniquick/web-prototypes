Description

abstract · LIBNAVON(3)

libnavon exposes a 2nd-year B.Tech Computer Science student at Manipal and Linux kernel contributor (mainline + stable) as a linkable systems library. The header declares three subsystems — kernel, security, and ml — behind a single stable entry point.

Head of a CTF team with a national #1 and international top-10 finishes. The call graph spans vulnerability research through to reinforcement learning: from crawling leaked keys across GitHub to teaching a network to play poker on a laptop GPU. Thread-safe under exam-season load (best effort).
